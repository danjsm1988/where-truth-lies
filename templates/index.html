What Where the Truth Lies Does

Most political analysis flattens complex claims into a single verdict. Where the Truth Lies splits compound claims into parts, separates documented facts from interpretation, finds the root concern underneath the rhetoric, identifies where genuine disagreement stops being factual and becomes a dispute over values, priorities, or constitutional meaning, and then looks for any real common ground that still exists.

ClaimLab is the live engine. Submit any political claim in the sidebar. We do not tell you what to think. We show you what is actually there.
