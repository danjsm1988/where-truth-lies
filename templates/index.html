Methodology

What this platform is actually doing.

Most political analysis collapses complex claims into a single verdict and leaves the user with the illusion of certainty. Where the Truth Lies is designed to do the opposite. It separates compound claims into parts, distinguishes documented facts from inference, surfaces what the rhetoric leaves out, identifies the real value conflict beneath the argument, and only then asks whether any real common ground exists.

This platform is also being built around a stricter trust rule: it cannot confidently contradict recent reality. Reality anchors exist because that failure mode is existential. Trust foundation comes before all future expansion.

Layer 06 Standard

Common Ground must be narrow, specific, and real. It must be grounded in what the documented facts support, lawful within the relevant constitutional and institutional framework, practically achievable through existing institutions without waiting for either side to win a political argument, and likely to produce better measurable outcomes for citizens than continued partisan deadlock. It is not the midpoint between two talking points. It is not a rhetorical gesture toward unity. Do not manufacture false consensus. If no genuine common ground exists in the documented record, state that explicitly.

Trust Direction

Reality anchors are the current patch for high-risk claims. Long term, the platform is headed toward a live-source tripwire model so current or contested claims can be checked against fresh discussion and sourced context before excavation analysis is finalized.

Live Grounded
When triggered, the system performs a live retrieval of current public information before analysis begins. Verified findings are injected as a factual baseline.

This does not determine truth. It establishes what is currently documented so the excavation does not operate on outdated or fabricated context. Claims marked in green are Live Grounded. Claims without this marker were not live-verified at the time of analysis.

If no reliable live information is found, the system proceeds using existing knowledge and marks uncertainty accordingly.
